About Me

I hold a Master's in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Oulu, with a distinguished academic record and expertise spanning AI, embedded systems, and human-computer interaction. My research focuses on integrating theoretical frameworks—such as structured argumentation, critical thinking models, and cognitive scaffolding—with Large Language Models to create intelligent B2B and Decision Support Systems.


With over two years of hands-on experience in machine learning and a strong background in VHDL and system design, I specialize in implementing deep learning models on GPUs, particularly within Machine Learning as a Service (MLaaS) contexts. My work bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI research and applied machine learning, translating theory into robust, scalable tools.


When I'm not immersed in research, you'll find me at the gym, hiking through nature, or listening to thought-provoking podcasts that fuel my passion for critical thinking and mathematical reasoning.
